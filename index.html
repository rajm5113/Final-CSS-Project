Wintertime in Bangalore

Wintertime in Bangalore is a beautiful time. Even though it can be chilly at times, it is nice to walk with my peers. This site includes some pictures I have taken on these walks. Sometimes we walk around the neighborhood, other times we make it to the woods. benglore also has some beautiful lakes and rivers including the Ulsoor (Near M.G. Road) and theCubbon Park.
